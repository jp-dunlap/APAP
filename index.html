Arkansas Palestinian Accountability Project

Systematically investigating, documenting, and analyzing all economic, political, and cultural ties between Arkansas entities and the State of Israel to identify any form of complicity in the oppression of the Palestinian people.

Explore the Connections

Our Areas of Investigation

APAP's research is structured around five interconnected themes to create a comprehensive map of complicity.

Economic & Financial Ties

Following the money trail of complicity through state investments, corporate contracts, and bilateral trade agreements.

Political & Legislative Ties

Mapping state-sanctioned support for apartheid through anti-BDS laws, pro-Israel resolutions, and lobbying networks.

Academic & Institutional Ties

Uncovering complicity in higher education through university partnerships, endowment investments, and censored discourse.

Military, Security & Law Enforcement

Exposing the local-global nexus of repression, from arms production to law enforcement exchange programs.

Cultural & Ideological Ties

Investigating how consent for apartheid is manufactured through Christian Zionism, media efforts, and propaganda.

Pillar Case Studies

These foundational analyses demonstrate how all threads of complicity are woven together through dominant nexuses of power.

The Walton Nexus

Architect of Exploitation, Pillar of Apartheid

An investigation into how Walmart Inc. and the Walton Family Foundation operate as an integrated apparatus, systemically enabling the Israeli colonial-apartheid project through global commerce, complicit suppliers, and the manufacturing of ideological consent.

The Healthcare-Apartheid Nexus

Pillars of Healing, Foundations of Oppression

An analysis of how Arkansas's healthcare and pension systems are entangled with the Israeli apartheid economy through pharmaceutical procurement from companies like Teva and war-profiteering investments by UAMS, Baptist Health, and ATRS.
