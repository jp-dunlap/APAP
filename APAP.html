Project APAP

Investigating Arkansas-Israel Connections & Complicity

Research Snapshot: May 31, 2025

Our Mission

To systematically investigate, comprehensively document, critically analyze, and ruthlessly expose any and all ties (economic, financial, political, legislative, academic, institutional, military, security, cultural, or ideological) between entities based in or significantly operating within Arkansas and the State of Israel.

The paramount focus is identifying and substantiating any form of complicity in the ongoing, systematic oppression, dispossession, and potential genocide of the Palestinian people.

Guiding Framework: All operations are EXCLUSIVELY governed by the foundational principles and analytical rigor of PRAXIS_CORE_DIRECTIVES_V3.1 – embodying revolutionary leftist activism, anti-Zionism, and anti-imperialism.

APAP-Control Hub: The Engine of Inquiry

The APAP-Control Hub is an AI persona designated to orchestrate, strategically guide, and ideologically ground the research efforts of Project APAP.

Analyze Intelligence

Incisevely analyze briefings and research reports to identify connections and strategic gaps.

Craft Research Prompts

Generate detailed, targeted "Deep Research Prompts" for deployment with fresh AI instances.

Suggest Key Sources

Recommend specific documents, reports, and datasets to ground research in credible evidence.

Validate Findings

Critically review research reports for ideological and factual accuracy, providing directive feedback.

Drive Iterative Research

Guide the escalating process of inquiry, building a comprehensive map of complicity.

The Research Cycle: A Disciplined Approach

1

Analyze Initial Briefing: Control Hub scrutinizes foundational documents (e.g., "Arkansas Political Power Map") to identify key entities and potential ties.

2

Generate Prompts & Sources: Control Hub crafts 3-5 specific "Deep Research Prompts" and suggests 2-4 corroborating source types for each.

3

User Executes Research: User deploys a prompt with a fresh AI instance (e.g., Gemini Advanced 2.5 Pro), providing suggested sources to compile a "Deep Research Report."

4

Control Hub Reviews Report: The generated report is submitted to APAP-Control Hub for rigorous ideological and factual validation.

5

Directive Feedback: Control Hub provides concise feedback: APPROVE, DENY/REQUEST REVISION, or SUGGEST EDITS.

6

Synthesize & Iterate: Approved findings are synthesized. Control Hub generates new, more advanced prompts to deepen the inquiry, repeating the cycle.

Key Areas of Investigation

Political & Legislative

Academic & Institutional

University collaborations/investments, think tanks, foundations, cultural institutions.

Military & Security

Arkansas-based arms/security tech production, law enforcement/National Guard exchanges with Israeli security.

Cultural & Ideological

Influence of Christian Zionism, PR/Hasbara efforts, media narratives, conflation of anti-Zionism with antisemitism.
